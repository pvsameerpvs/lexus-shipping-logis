Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our air cargo, break bulk, heavy lift & Ro-Ro movements, and land transport services.

How can I place an order for your services?

Orders can be placed by contacting our customer service team via email at lexusshipping@gmail.com or by calling +971 58 234 8995. Our team will assist you in arranging the appropriate transportation solution for your cargo.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept various payment methods, including credit/debit cards, bank transfers, and other secure payment options. For detailed information, please refer to our net banking page.

Do you offer international shipping?

Yes, we offer international shipping services for both air and sea cargo. Our extensive network allows us to handle shipments to and from various countries worldwide.

What is your return policy?

Our return policy varies depending on the nature of the service provided. For detailed information, please contact our customer service team at lexusshipping@gmail.com
