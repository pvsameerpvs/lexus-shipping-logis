Our ServiceS

At Lexus Shipping we specialize in international freight forwarding, project cargo logistics, air chartering, and cross-trade movements. Our services are complemented by customs brokerage, door-to-door delivery, and multinational transport operations. We also offer end-to-end solutions, including secure storage & logistics and liner shipping & husbanding agency services, ensuring seamless, compliant, and cost-efficient global cargo management.

International Freight Forwarding

Expert coordination of global shipments via air, sea, or land. We handle documentation, customs compliance, and end-to-end logistics for seamless, reliable cargo movement.

Project Cargo Movements

Specialized handling of oversized, heavy, or complex shipments for industrial projects. Tailored solutions include engineering support, route planning, and regulatory compliance.

Air Chartering

Dedicated aircraft services for urgent, high-value, or sensitive cargo. Flexible, fast, and secure global air freight solutions tailored to your timelines.

Cross Trade Movements

Third-country logistics management, connecting shipments between two nations without direct origin involvement. Streamlined routing to reduce costs and delays.

Customs Brokerage & Delivery

Efficient customs clearance and compliance across borders. We manage duties, documentation, and last-mile delivery for hassle-free import/export.

Door to Door Service

End-to-end logistics from pickup to final delivery. Real-time tracking and multi-modal transport ensure cargo arrives safely, on time, anywhere.

Multinational Transport Operations

Seamless coordination of cross-border logistics. Leverage our global network and local expertise to navigate regulations, languages, and cultural nuances.

Storage & Logistics

Secure warehousing, inventory management, and distribution. Our facilities offer climate control, real-time tracking, and value-added services like packing/labeling.

Liner Shipping & Husbanding Agency

Regular container shipping via global liner routes paired with comprehensive port agency support: crew assistance, bunkering, and port coordination.
